Lamborghini Aventador Ultimae Unveiled, Farewell V12

Lamborghini has unveiled the Aventador LP 780-4 Ultimae, the last naturally-aspirated V12 production model from the Italian brand. - By Akash Bangera on june 22, 2018

It combines sharp looks of the Aventador S and the performance of the SVJ and will be produced in a numbered series of 350 units in coupe and 250 roadster editions. This will be the last time Lambo will be using its 6.5-litre V12 engine. In the Aventador Ultimae, it produces 780 PS and 720 Nm of torque. It will be mated to the familiar 7-speed transmission which sends the motor’s power to all 4 wheels.

Ford Endeavour Titanium Trim Discontinued, SUV Priced From Rs. 33.80 Lakhs

Ford has revised the variant lineup of the Endeavour in July 2021 and the SUV’s asking price now begins from Rs. 33.80 lakhs (ex-showroom, Delhi). - By Akshatha Yaji on june 30, 2018

The base Titanium 4×2 variant that was retailed for Rs. 29.99 lakhs (ex-showroom, Delhi) is gone and thus, the SUV is offered in only 2 variants – Titanium+ (in both 4×2 and 4×4 forms) and Sport – now. This has resulted in the SUV being offered with all the bells and whistles right from the base variant. As standard, it comes with features such as 8-inch infotainment touchscreen, smartphone connectivity, 8-way power adjustable front seats, handsfree power tailgate, panoramic sunroof, semi-auto parallel park assist and cruise control, to name a few.
